A few terms worth knowing

  • Digital HR — using technology and data to run and improve people processes, and to make HR a faster, more evidence-based partner to the business.
  • Industry 4.0 — the current wave of automation, AI, data, and connected systems reshaping work and the skills it requires.
  • Reskilling / upskilling — helping people learn new capabilities (reskilling) or deepen existing ones (upskilling) as roles evolve.
  • Employer branding — an organisation’s reputation as a place to work, increasingly shaped in public and by employees themselves.
  • Hybrid / work-from-anywhere — work models that combine or remove fixed-location constraints, changing how culture is sustained.
  • Generative AI assistant — a tool (such as Claude, Gemini, or ChatGPT) that produces text and other content to support — not replace — human work.
