A few terms worth knowing

  • Strategic HRM (SHRM) — aligning people strategy with business strategy so HR decisions serve the organisation’s objectives.
  • Talent management — a connected approach to attracting, retaining, and developing the capabilities the strategy needs.
  • Change management — helping people move from a current way of working to a new one, with engagement and capability.
  • Learning organization — one that continuously acquires, shares, and applies knowledge as a routine strength.
  • M&A / joint venture — mergers, acquisitions, and partnerships where talent and culture integration drive whether value is created.
  • Corporate governance — the system of accountability, ethics, and oversight by which an organisation is directed and controlled.
