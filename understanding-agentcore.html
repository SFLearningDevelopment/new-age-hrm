A few terms worth knowing

  • Role priming — telling the AI who to act as, to set the right lens.
  • Context loading (R-C-T-C-F) — supplying role, context, task, constraints, and format.
  • Few-shot — giving examples or explicit criteria so the AI follows your standard.
  • Chain-of-thought — asking the AI to show its reasoning step by step.
  • Structured output — requesting a table, template, or fixed layout.
  • Guardrail prompting — constraints like “flag if unsure” to curb fabrication and bias.
